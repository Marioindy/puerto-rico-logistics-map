# Important Airport Map Fields for Selection

## Core Physical Specifications and Infrastructure

### **Basic Facility Information**[^1]

**General Airport Data**

- Airport name and ICAO/IATA codes[^2][^3]
- Airport type and classification (international, domestic, regional)[^4][^5]
- Geographic coordinates (latitude, longitude, elevation)[^3][^6]
- Operating hours and timezone[^3]
- Land area and total airport boundary[^6][^5]
- Airport reference point (ARP) coordinates[^6][^3]

**Airside Infrastructure**[^5][^6]

- Runway specifications (length, width, surface type, load capacity)[^2][^3][^6]
- Runway markings and lighting systems[^7][^8]
- Taxiway networks and guidance systems[^9][^5][^6]
- Apron and aircraft parking positions[^10][^5][^6]
- Aircraft gates and boarding bridge configurations[^11][^10]
- Ground support equipment (GSE) staging areas[^12][^13]


### **Terminal Complex and Buildings**[^14][^6]

**Terminal Buildings and Structures**[^15][^14][^6]

- Terminal building dimensions and floor area[^15][^14]
- Number of levels and vertical circulation systems[^14]
- Concourse layouts and gate assignments[^10][^11]
- Passenger processing areas (check-in, security, immigration)[^16][^14]
- Baggage handling system layouts and capacities[^17][^18][^19]
- Retail, dining, and service facility locations[^20][^21][^22]
- Administrative offices and airline operations centers[^15][^14]
- Air traffic control tower location[^23][^24]

**Specialized Buildings and Facilities**[^6][^15]

- Hangars and aircraft maintenance facilities[^4][^6]
- Cargo terminals and freight handling buildings[^13][^12]
- Emergency services stations (fire, medical, police)[^24][^23]
- Ground service equipment storage facilities[^6]
- Customs and immigration facilities[^16][^14]


## **Transportation and Access Systems**

### **Ground Transportation Infrastructure**[^25][^26]

**Parking Facilities**[^26][^27][^25]

- Daily parking lots (short-term, long-term, premium)[^25][^26]
- Parking capacity by zone and pricing structure[^26][^25]
- Electric vehicle charging stations[^26]
- Employee parking areas[^1][^25]
- Rental car facilities and locations[^16][^26]
- Parking garage specifications and levels[^27][^25][^26]

**Ground Access Systems**[^28][^4]

- Airport access roads and traffic flow patterns[^29][^4]
- Public transportation connections (rail, subway, bus)[^28][^16]
- Taxi and rideshare pickup/drop-off zones[^25][^16]
- Cell phone waiting lots[^30][^25]
- Service vehicle access routes[^4]


## **Operational and Service Systems**

### **Security and Safety Infrastructure**[^31][^23][^24]

**Security Systems and Zones**[^31][^23]

- Security checkpoint locations and configurations[^14][^16]
- Restricted access areas and perimeter security[^23][^31]
- CCTV coverage and monitoring systems[^31][^15]
- Emergency response routes and evacuation procedures[^32][^24][^23]
- Grid mapping systems for emergency response[^32][^23]

**Emergency Services and Safety**[^24][^23]

- Fire station locations and response capabilities[^23][^24]
- Emergency medical facilities and equipment[^23]
- Emergency vehicle staging areas[^24][^32]
- Disaster response and mass casualty areas[^23]
- Emergency communication systems[^24][^23]


### **Utility and Support Systems**

**Power and Electrical Infrastructure**[^7][^9]

- Electrical distribution systems and substations[^7]
- Airfield lighting systems (runway, taxiway, approach)[^8][^9][^7]
- Navigation aid installations and lighting[^9][^8]
- Emergency power systems and backup generators[^33][^7]
- Ground lighting system management[^9]

**HVAC and Environmental Systems**[^34][^35][^33]

- Central utility plants and distribution[^34][^33]
- Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning zones[^35][^33][^34]
- Chilled water and steam distribution systems[^33][^34]
- Environmental control system layouts[^35][^33]
- Energy management and monitoring systems[^33][^35]

**Communication and Data Systems**[^36][^37][^38]

- Air traffic control communication systems[^38][^36]
- Airport operational communication networks[^37][^36]
- Digital signage and passenger information systems[^37]
- Data center and IT infrastructure locations[^38][^37]
- Wireless network coverage and capacity[^36][^38]


## **Specialized Aviation Systems**

### **Fuel Management Infrastructure**[^39][^40]

**Fuel System Components**[^41][^40][^39]

- Fuel storage tank farms and capacities[^40][^39]
- Fuel distribution pipeline networks[^39][^40]
- Aircraft refueling positions and hydrant systems[^40][^39]
- Fuel quality control and testing facilities[^42][^39]
- Fuel management system control centers[^39][^40]

**Fuel Safety and Environmental**[^40][^39]

- Fuel containment and spill prevention systems[^40]
- Environmental monitoring equipment[^40]
- Fire suppression systems for fuel areas[^40]
- Fuel waste and recycling facilities[^40]


### **Cargo and Freight Operations**[^12][^13]

**Cargo Facility Infrastructure**[^13][^12]

- Cargo terminal buildings and processing areas[^12][^13]
- Cargo aircraft parking aprons[^13][^12]
- Truck docking facilities and staging areas[^12][^13]
- Cargo screening and security systems[^13]
- Cold storage and specialized cargo handling[^13]
- Customs inspection and bonded storage areas[^12][^13]

**Ground Handling Equipment Areas**[^12][^13]

- GSE maintenance and storage facilities[^12]
- Equipment charging and fueling stations[^12]
- Baggage handling equipment staging[^17][^12]


## **Navigation and Approach Systems**

### **Airspace and Navigation Infrastructure**[^43][^3][^9]

**Navigation Aids and Equipment**[^43][^3][^9]

- Instrument landing systems (ILS) installations[^38][^9]
- VOR/DME navigation equipment locations[^9][^38]
- Radar installations and coverage areas[^38]
- Approach lighting systems[^8][^9]
- Weather monitoring equipment[^3][^38]

**Airspace Mapping Data**[^44][^43][^3]

- Terminal maneuvering area boundaries[^45][^46]
- UAS facility maps and altitude restrictions[^44]
- Approach and departure procedure zones[^46][^45]
- Aircraft movement and trajectory data[^47][^48]


## **Passenger Services and Amenities**

### **Commercial and Service Areas**[^21][^22][^20]

**Retail and Food Service**[^22][^20][^21]

- Restaurant and café locations by terminal area[^20][^21][^22]
- Retail shop locations and categories[^21][^22][^20]
- Service facility locations (banks, lounges, spas)[^20][^21]
- Duty-free shopping areas[^22][^21]
- Business centers and meeting facilities[^20]

**Passenger Comfort and Accessibility**[^21][^14]

- Rest areas and seating configurations[^14][^21]
- Family and child care facilities[^16][^14]
- Accessibility features and ADA compliance areas[^14]
- Pet relief areas and services[^16][^14]
- Medical facilities and first aid stations[^20][^14]


## **Environmental and Sustainability Features**

### **Environmental Systems and Monitoring**[^49]

**Environmental Infrastructure**[^49]

- Stormwater management systems[^49]
- Waste processing and recycling facilities[^49]
- Air quality monitoring stations[^49]
- Noise monitoring equipment locations[^49]
- Environmental impact zones[^49]

**Sustainability Features**

- Solar panel installations and renewable energy systems
- Green building certifications and sustainable design features
- Electric vehicle infrastructure
- Energy-efficient lighting and HVAC systems
- Water conservation and recycling systems


## **Technology and Digital Infrastructure**

### **Smart Airport Systems**[^50][^47]

**Digital Mapping and Analytics**[^47][^50][^49]

- Real-time airport monitoring systems[^50][^49]
- Passenger flow tracking and analytics[^14]
- Aircraft movement prediction systems[^48][^47]
- Digital twin and 3D modeling data[^50][^15]
- IoT sensor networks and data collection points[^33]

**Advanced Technologies**[^50][^49]

- Satellite imagery integration for mapping[^50][^49]
- AI-powered operational optimization systems[^49]
- Automated baggage handling systems[^18][^17]
- Biometric identification and processing areas[^14]
- Autonomous ground vehicle pathways[^50]
